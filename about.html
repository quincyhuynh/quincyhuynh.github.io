About

I am an incoming PhD student in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science department at University of California, Berkeley. My interests are in radio-frequency and analog circuit design. I currently do research under the guidance of Professor Steven Conolly in the BioE/EECS Department.

I graduated from UC Berkeley with a M.S in EECS in Spring 2018 and a B.S. in EECS in Spring 2017. My main coursework was in integrated circuits and systems. I spent a large part of my undergraduate career as a teaching assistant, and I've continued to teach as a graduate student instructor.
